What will my finances look like in a year?

If you spend ₹38,000/month on a ₹45,000 salary and expenses grow just 2% monthly (lifestyle creep), in 12 months your monthly expenses will reach ₹42,800 — leaving you with barely ₹2,000/month to save. Small drift compounds into big problems.

How do I predict my future savings?

Take your current savings, add monthly surplus (income minus expenses), and subtract any expected increases in spending. The gap between your projection and your goal is your wake-up call.
